Build A Blog on Git-Page with Hexo -- (1)

- Preface

Building a blog and writing articles to share what you learned recently with people on internet, is always a practical method for developers to record their track of learning, this is the reason why I decided to give it a go, starting to build one. Meanwhile, there are serveral reasons I recommend you can have your own blog on gh-pages of github.

  1. It’s free to have your site within 300MB.
  2. It’s on github, this means it will be easier to find solutions when you are dealing issues.
  3. You can write your article in Markdown, super easy since it is the simplified version fo WYSIWYG(what your see is what you get).

Before starting, I’ve searched for a while for choosing the framework, I found that Hexo is famous for easy-to-use, light-weight building, therefore, I think there is no reason for me not to use it.

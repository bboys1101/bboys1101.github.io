Implementing DoubleClick Floodlight Tags

2016-10-19



What’s Floodlight Tags?

A Floodlight tag is an HTML tag that you place on your web site to track conversions, such as a consumer making a purchase or completing an online form.

How to implementing it?

Basically, there are 2 different kinds of Floodlight Tags, one tag is called to track visits to a single page, another tag to track clicks on a button leading to an external page.

First of all, there are 3 mothods to track button claick.

  1. Cilck opens a new window (callFloodlight_new).
  2. Click loads a page in the same window (callFloodlight_same).
  3. A Image Floodlight which is only used in certain circumstances (“callStaticFL”).

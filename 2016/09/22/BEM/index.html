Learning BEM

2016-09-22
#BEM

What is BEM?

  1. Helping developers to organize and maintain the css in a more efficient way.
  2. Designed to fix non-semantic DOM structures at all levels (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, templates and UX design)

Why Learning?

Basic:

  1. Block: A block is an independent entity with its own meaning. (heading, button, navigation)
  2. Element: Part of a block, tied to it semantically and functionally. It has no meaning outside of the block it belongs to.
  3. Modifier: Modifiers are flags set on blocks or elements; they define properties or states.

How does BEM work?

  1. Providing the reusability, making reusable css code.

Why use EBM?

  • Reduce the conflict of specificity
  • Free the structural dependencies.
  • The modularity can avoid problems form cascading.
